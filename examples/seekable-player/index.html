Piano Roll

X = time, Y = pitch. The red vertical line is the playhead. Notes are colored by channel; ch 10 (drums) is gray.

Channel Notes

X = key number (0–127), Y = MIDI channel (1–16). Active notes light up in their channel color.

Waveform / Spectrum

Fill = spectrum (frequency domain), line = waveform (time domain).
